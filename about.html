About

Prashant Kashyap.

I design enterprise software for the people who can't quit it.

Customers of consumer apps have infinite alternatives. A Customer Service Representative who sits inside Oracle's service-activation platform for eight hours a day does not. She learns the quirks, endures the misfires, and develops workarounds her manager will never see. My work is about noticing what she's carrying and redesigning the system underneath it.

Currently I'm a Senior UX Designer at Oracle Communications, leading UX for two Redwood modernizations — ASAP (service activation) and Unified Assurance (network monitoring). Before Oracle I spent two years in Siemens' Smart Buildings division designing components for the Element design system; a year at McDermott on offshore oil & gas project tracking; and, earlier, roles at InfoBeans, Mobisoft, and the startup Conexstra — where Kexplora made NDTV's Top 20 Hottest Startups list in 2015.

Alongside the design work I co-founded Exaltic Nutrition, a sports-nutrition brand that served IPL and Indian state-level cricket players from 2016 to 2019. Running a company while holding a full-time design role teaches you things a design career alone cannot — what it costs to ship, what it means to own an outcome, how to lead across functions that don't share your vocabulary.

I'm IxDF-trained, HFI Certified Usability Analyst, and based in Pune. I work in English and Hindi, and I'm open to global roles.
